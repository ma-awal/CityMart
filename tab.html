about us

Mission : Our mission is to serve our customers with unparalleled products and services and ensure quality.
Vision: Our vision is to set a benchmark on consumers’ minds so that CitiMart becomes synonymous with Authenticity, Originality and Quality.

CitiMart is an Omni-channel retail megastore that operates both Online and Offline. Currently, we have a physical store situated at Kakoli, Dhaka. CitiMart is enhanced with a digital platform to provide sophisticated deliveries to a few places inside Dhaka. We are also working to provide delivery to the entire Dhaka City. We collect goods from verified and authentic suppliers.

Launched in 2021, CitiMart uses the technological and operational infrastructures provided by the parent company BYSL Global Technology Group, a next-generation technology conglomerate.
